  • Ghana Tech and Business Hubs' Database System

    Business and technology hubs are developing trend globally to ensure entrepreneurs, business start-ups, self– employed people and small business owners have a low-cost option in creating, developing and maintaining a sustainable business. This database project is created to track the generic innovation and entrepreneurial ecosystem enablers across Ghana (Ghana Technology and Business Hubs Network). Member hubs and affiliates must be legally registered organisations or businesses based in Ghana to be part of the group. The hub should have a physical facility for gathering its target groups. The group has business meetings and various committees to excute agendas as per agreed. Individual hubs have partners both in Ghana and outside with each having specific customer segments to serve. The database seeks to manage its operations and monitor the performance and relationship between the hubs in the network and also and to lead the development of a cohesive and strengthened tech and business eco-system in Ghana.

  • Elpidia Foundation

    Elpidia is an organization that seeks to promote the use of technology and innovative approaches to learning and teaching all over Africa. Elpidia is the Greek word for hope. We are convinced in the potential that resides in Africa’s youth, and we look to the future with hope. Never has there been a point in history, when so many people could be empowered with tools to change the world for good in such inexpensive and effective ways. The spider in Elpidia’s logo represents our believe that as a people, as a civilization, we can build our way to any destination we set our eyes to. Elpidia provides primary and high schools with a tool that helps teachers and administrators to record student progress, with a host of easy-to-use data analysis tools that help teachers to analyse student performance on a personal and group bases. The app greatly eases the rather painstaking process of recording students progress while encouraging every teacher to interact with technology.
